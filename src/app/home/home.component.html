A collection of my sketches made with p5.js

Flood Fill
For resting a flood fill algorithm.
Falling Sand
A simple falling sand simulation.
Langton's Ant
A two-dimensional Turing machine. See Wikipedia.
Firefly Messages
Particles forming words. Inspired by the helpful messages in the game "Oddworld: Abe's Oddysee".
Epicycles
Epicycle animations.
2D Noise with HSL
Exploring the HSL color space with 2d Perlin noise while trying to get a wide color distribution.
Perlin Noise Flow Field
A Perlin noise flow field based on the video Coding Challenge #24: Perlin Noise Flow Field by The Coding Train.
Colorbands
An experiment with HSL colorspace and trying to get a uniform color distribution from normal distributed `noise()`.
2D Noise
Experiments with 2D Perlin noise based on the video I.5: 2D Noise - Perlin Noise and p5.js Tutorial by The Coding Train.
1D Noise Graph
An animated graph of 1D Perlin noise based on the video I.4: Graphing 1D Perlin Noise - Perlin Noise and p5.js Tutorial by The Coding Train.
Collatz Conjecture
A visualization of the Collatz conjecture based on the video Coding the Collatz Conjecture by The Coding Train. Takes a moment to load.
